## How to Play (30 Second Tutorial)

### Desktop
1. **Click and hold** on the bird in the slingshot
2. **Drag backwards** to aim (see the white dotted line)
3. **Release** to launch
4. **Destroy all pigs** to win!

### Mobile
1. **Touch and hold** the bird
2. **Drag backwards** to aim
3. **Release** to fire
4. **Destroy all pigs** to win!
